Two Changes Applied (BankNifty Only)

Fix 1 — Skip 10:00–10:30 Slot

14 trades in that slot
WR = 28.6%  |  Net = −856 pts
Worst 30-min window out of all 7 slots

Post-open volatility in BankNifty hasn't settled by 10:00. The Supertrend indicator makes a flip during the first 30 min of the "mid-morning" phase — entries taken immediately on the flip get caught by the counter-move.

New config: BNKN_SKIP_SLOT_START = "10:00"
New config: BNKN_SKIP_SLOT_END = "10:30"

Fix 2 — RSI BUY Upper: 65→60

RSI 62–65 BUY zone
12 trades  |  WR = 41.7%  |  Net = −87 pts

BankNifty overbought dynamics differ from Nifty — RSI above 60 on a BUY entry is already exhausted momentum. Nifty retains RSI_BUY_HIGH = 65 (performs well at 75% WR in 59–62 zone).

New config: RSI_BUY_HIGH_BANKNIFTY = 60
Nifty unchanged: RSI_BUY_HIGH = 65
