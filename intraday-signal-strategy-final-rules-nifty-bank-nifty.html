Signal Entry Rules

✦ BUY Signal

1
Supertrend → GREEN Price above Supertrend line (7, 3)
2
EMA Crossover → Bullish EMA(9) above EMA(21)
3
RSI → 50 to 70 Momentum rising, not overbought
4
VWAP → Price ABOVE VWAP NEW Institutional bias is bullish; removes false longs

✦ SELL Signal

1
Supertrend → RED Price below Supertrend line (7, 3)
2
EMA Crossover → Bearish EMA(9) below EMA(21)
3
RSI → 30 to 50 Momentum falling, not oversold
4
VWAP → Price BELOW VWAP NEW Institutional bias is bearish; removes false shorts
