The single biggest separator: Hold duration. Winners average 193 min, losers average 67 min — a 3x difference. This is not a coincidence. Winners are trades that the Supertrend never reverses on. Losers are trades the market immediately disagrees with and SL-hits within the hour. RSI and ADX at entry are nearly identical between winners and losers — they do not predict outcome. Only whether the trade survives the first 60–90 minutes matters.

The 2-hour threshold is the single most predictive rule in the entire strategy:
Any trade still alive after 120 minutes (2 hours from entry) has a 61–100% chance of being a winner. Any trade closed before 60 minutes is essentially a guaranteed loss (0–12% WR). What this means: The strategy cannot improve by exiting losers faster — they already exit fast via SL. The problem is that the SL is hit within minutes on bad entries. The Supertrend SL correctly protects the good trades (they survive and grow). No additional exit rule is needed.
