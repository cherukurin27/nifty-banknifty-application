The Single Biggest Insight

Winners hold 216 min on average. Losers exit in 82 min.
The system makes money by staying in winning trades, not by having a high win rate. Win rate is only 41% — but big wins (avg +131–397 pts) vastly outweigh small losses (avg -45–140 pts).
