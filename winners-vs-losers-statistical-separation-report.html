The ST Gap% — Best Entry-Time Separator (Bimodal Distribution)

The bimodal pattern is the key insight: Tight gap (<0.10%) = fresh entry right as ST flips — SL is close, trail hasn't started, but the trend is clean. Wide gap (>0.20%) = trade entered after ST already moved away — trailing SL is already locked above entry = genuine trend momentum. Middle gap (0.15–0.25% NIFTY SELL / 0.25–0.30% BNKN SELL) = the kill zone where SL is near the hard cap — any retracement = full loss, but trail hasn't built enough to protect.

This means any simple "gap threshold" will cut both good zones. The right filter is: block the specific middle band, not a blanket cap.
